War Doctrine: Systems Thinking

Designing for Failure

Assume tools break and responses degrade. The architecture is judged by fallback behavior, not happy-path demos.

Cost vs Intelligence

Routing every task to the largest model is lazy engineering. I treat model selection as a constrained optimization problem.

Explainability Caution

Explanations can hide weak assumptions. I pair SHAP with stress tests and threshold audits.

Monitoring Beyond Accuracy

Track latency, drift, and user correction loops. Production health is operational, not academic.
